Elon Musk, born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa, is a visionary entrepreneur known for founding and leading several groundbreaking companies. He is the CEO of SpaceX, which aims to revolutionize space travel and colonize Mars, and Tesla, Inc., a pioneer in electric vehicles and renewable energy solutions. Musk also acquired Twitter in 2022, rebranding it as X, and is involved in other ventures like Neuralink, The Boring Company, and xAI. His ambitious projects and innovative approach have made him one of the wealthiest individuals globally, with a net worth estimated at $247 billion as of August 2024.
